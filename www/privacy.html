🧘 Zen Wallpapers - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Zen Wallpapers. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application handles your information.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Zen Wallpapers is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No account registration required
  • No personal data collection
  • No tracking or analytics
  • No advertising SDKs

3. Permissions We Request

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to download wallpaper images from our servers.
  • Set Wallpaper: Required to set your chosen image as your device wallpaper.

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and nothing else.

4. Image Sources

Wallpapers displayed in the app are sourced from Unsplash, a platform for freely-usable images. All images are licensed under the Unsplash License and are free to use.

5. Data Storage

Zen Wallpapers stores data only locally on your device:

  • Downloaded wallpapers are cached temporarily for offline viewing
  • No data is transmitted to external servers (except to download images)
  • You can clear all cached data through your device settings

6. Third-Party Services

The app connects to:

  • Unsplash API: To fetch high-quality wallpaper images. Unsplash's privacy policy applies to their service: Unsplash Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages: To host our curated wallpaper collection

7. Children's Privacy

Zen Wallpapers is rated for Everyone and does not collect any personal information from users of any age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

📧 Email: support@cloude.dev

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all local data.

Zen Wallpapers is developed by Cloude. We believe in transparency and respecting user privacy.
